The Crusade of Our Time

“

The true soldier fights not because he hates what is in front of him, but because he loves what is behind him.

”

—G.K. Chesterton, Illustrated London News, Jan. 14, 1911

Everyone agrees there's a problem with society

When it comes to politics, common folk everywhere are in agreement that society is engulfed in chaos, injustice reigns supreme, and that the lawless are making the laws.

There remains yet one solution

Amidst the chaos, the timeless teaching of Mother Church can be heard echoing faintly from the past…

“

We tried to warn you!

”

Source: Timeless Teaching of the Church.

Step one

The first step to restoring the tranquility of order, is for the Church to do as she was commanded by her King: Proclaim the Truth. (“Teach them to observe all things I have commanded you.”—Matt. 28:20.)

However, in the primordial combat between Good and Evil, before our bishops and priests can courageously lead us into battle under the standard of Christ the King, they first need to know that we have their back.

Step zero

So, therefore, prudence demands that the all-important work of reforming society must begin by the laity formally stating their needs and desires to their hierarchy.

The is, at its core, a lay petition to the Roman Catholic hierarchy (and, by extension, all clergy and religious) to preach the Social Kingship of Christ.

CODE OF CANON LAW, Canon 212

  • §2. The Christian faithful are free to make known to the pastors of the Church their needs, especially spiritual ones, and their desires.
  • §3. Conscious of their own responsibility, Christ’s faithful have the right, indeed at times the duty, in keeping with their knowledge, competence, and position, to manifest to the sacred pastors their views on matters which concern the good of the Church.
    • They have the right also to make their views known to others of Christ’s faithful.
    • In doing so they must always respect the integrity of faith and morals, show due reverence to the sacred pastors, and take into account both the common good and the dignity of individuals.

Source: vatican.va

Sign the petition

You are 7½ minutes away from signing the petition!

It's really pretty straightforward and self-explanatory, but, just in case you need it, we have prepared these:

Step-by-Step Instructions
  1. Visit the Enroll page.
  2. Enter your best email address.
  3. Enter your cell phone number.
    • Every entry in this petition is verified using two unique forms of electronic authentication (email & cell), thereby making the authority of this petition unquestionable. The hierarchy will have no other choice than to acknowledge us.
    • We do not use the less secure Password-Based Authentication. This method ensures not only optimal security, but it is also easier for you to not have to remember yet another password.
    • Two forms of authentication (2FA) is considered the default standard in web security.
    • Please note that only cell phone numbers from the USA are accepted. If you live outside the USA, we regret that you cannot sign this petition. Please refer to the FAQ below if you would like to help bring this petition to your home nation.
  4. Press "Verify cell".
    • A unique code will be sent to your cell phone via text message.
    • Enter this unique code in the popup window.
  5. Press the "Enroll" button!
  6. You should then receive an email from CrusaderBot.
    • To confirm your email address, please click on the confirmation link in this email.
  7. You will then be logged in, and directed to your account Dashboard.
    • From there, you can submit your entry in the petition.
  8. Begin by entering your real full name.
    • In order for this petition to be taken seriously by the Apostolic Offices to which we are submitting it, all signees are required to submit their entry using their real name.
    • Your entry in this petition will be directly addressed to your bishop, and therefore must be endorsed using your real name.
    • Using an alias, therefore, is against our Terms of Service.
    • If you do not want to use your real name, please stop here, and forward this petition to your friends who will!
  9. Upload a picture of your face.
    • We are doing this for the same reasons given in Step 8 above; namely, so that we may be taken seriously by those to whom we submit this petition.
    • This petition will be far more impactful to our bishops if they are able to look upon, not simply our names, but also our faces.
  10. Optionally, you can enter a link to a page on the web where you appear; such as your business "About" page, or a social media profile.
    • We will remove links to random pages in which you are not clearly associated.
  11. Next, please accurately choose your home diocese or eparchy.
    • Your bishop will see all entries submitted to his diocese.
    • For the present, this petition is being submitted only to dioceses and eparchies in the USA, and to the Holy See.
    • If you live outside the USA, we regret that you cannot sign this petition. Please refer to the FAQ below if you would like to help bring this petition to your home diocese.
  12. Lastly, please enter a custom exhortation.
    • This is your opportunity to directly address your bishop with your needs and desires pertaining to the Church's role in society.
    • You are limited to 40 words.
    • We will promptly remove entries that do not show due reverence to the successors to the Apostles.
    • Entries that are not relevant to the scope of this petition will also be removed.
    • All entries are subject to edits for grammar and punctuation.
    • If you do not want to enter a custom exhortation, you are welcome to use the default text provided.
  13. Press the "Submit" button!
  14. Share with your family, friends, acquaintances… the world!

Answers to FAQs

  • In signing this petition, you are signing the Crusade Manifesto.
  • You will have the ability to edit or remove your entry at any time.
  • Presently, only Catholics in the USA can submit this petition.
    • If you live outside the USA, and would like to help deploy this petition in your home nation, please contact us.
